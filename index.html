Recent Work

Closing Time

HTML5 | CSS | Javascript | Google API

This website was created as a tool to allow the user to easily and rapidly determine the closing times of restaurants, bars, and coffee shops in their vicinity. The idea was born out of many personal experiences of being out at night with friends, and wishing there was a single place I could look to for this information. The program utilizes the HTML5 geolocation API and Google Maps/Places API. I am presently working on a mobile version that I hope to get into the App Store.

Social List

user research | personas | user flows | wireframing | prototyping

The Social List is a smartphone app that facilitates communication among students. It was designed to enable users to easily find and organize pertinent information while keeping them engaged in their community.

Library Checkout

observational research | wireframing

This project started out as a need-finding exercise to better understand how people interact with digital interfaces. I observed people at my local library use the electronic checkout kiosks with the goal of identifying any potential pain points in the process.
